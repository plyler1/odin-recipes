Salmon Recipe

Pan-Seared Salmon

Learn how to cook salmon using this pan-seared salmon recipe. The salmon slices are then seasoned with spices of choice and garnished with lemons.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon slices
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 slices lemon
  • Any other spices of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat a large heavy pan over medium heat for 3 minutes.
  2. Coat salmon slices with olive oil. Place skin-side down in the preheated pan and increase heat to high. Sprinkle with capers, salt, pepper, and other spices. Cook for 3 minutes on one side and turn slices over, cooking other side for about 5 minutes, or until salmon flakes easily.
  3. Transfer salmon over to plates and sprinkle with lemons.
