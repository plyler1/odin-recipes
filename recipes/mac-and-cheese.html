Mac and Cheese Recipe

Simple Macaroni and Cheese

Want to make mac and cheese fast and easy? Use this recipe to make cheap and tasty mac and cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 1⁄4 cup butter
  • 1⁄4 cup flour
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of lightly salted water. Cook macaroni is the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked but firm to bite (about 8 minutes).
  2. While doing step 1, melt butter in a saucepan on medium heat. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, which should take about 5 minutes. Pour in milk slowly, while continuously stirring. Continue cooking and stirring until the mixture is smooth and bubbling in about 5 minutes, making sure the milk doesn't burn.
  3. Add cheese and stir until melted, this takes about 2-4 minutes.
  4. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.
